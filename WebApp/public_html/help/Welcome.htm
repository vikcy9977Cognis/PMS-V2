Welcome

Welcome to the Online Help for the Dolphin Carrier System.

 

The contents of this help are work in process and will be continually extended during the project based on new documented knowledge of Dolphin's business experts, system analysts, programmers and last but not least the users.

 

The main content for the time being are:

  • Screen help for the Dolphin Carrier System  

  • Business Cases - Sample of specific business cases and how to act in that specific situation

  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

  • a glossary

  • full text search

  • a comprehensive index

 

Future content will also offer

  • training presentations

  • training movies

 

If you have any feedback then please voice it!
